While working at a company, I observed a very common but inefficient process: bicycle maintenance issues were being recorded on paper.
Besides generating unnecessary waste, this made it difficult to organize and track repair requests over time.
This inspired me to develop an app to replace this workflow with a more practical, digital, and sustainable solution. 🌱📱
The app centralizes bicycle repair requests, allowing users to easily log, organize, and manage issues,
improving workflow efficiency and reducing paper usage.
Technologies used:
• Swift
• SwiftUI
• MVVM architecture
• ObservableObject and @Published for state management
• Local data persistence (Manager for saving and loading requests)
This project helped me strengthen both my technical skills and my problem-solving mindset, with a 
strong focus on improving real-world processes through technology.
